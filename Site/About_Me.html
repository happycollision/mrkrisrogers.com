Kris Rogers

 
 

It may not seem like an obvious combination, but in many ways my career as a bass player and my pursuits as a photographer have intertwined nicely.


To put it simply: I’m all about making those I work with sound and look their best.


Whether it’s keeping time and making room for a vocalist on stage, or helping a client feel comfortable in front of the camera, at the end of the day I know I’ve done my best if you can walk away feeling you’ve achieved your best.
